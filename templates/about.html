About

Zomato is an online restaurant discovery and food delivery platform that provides usergenerated reviews and ratings for restaurants. Our project makes use of Machine Learning model to display ratings of a particular restaurant, given the required inputs.
Potential use case of Zomato rating prediction is to help restaurant owners improve their ratings by identifying areas for improvement.
Overall, the goal of Zomato rating prediction is to help restaurant owners improve their ratings and attract more customers, leading to increased revenue and business growth.

While we select the features and split the data for testing, we have to keep in mind that the data which is to be inputted on the website, wont be encoded and thus we have to create a pipeline and then save the model so that data which is present at hand gets encoded before serving as a input to the model
In this scenario, we use the machine learning model “Random Forest”, as it gives us the most relevant, realistic, and optimised results. When comparing with other models, Random forest outperforms other model by a very large margin and brings us the best accuracy
